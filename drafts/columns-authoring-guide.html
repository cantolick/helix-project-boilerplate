How to Author Columns in Google Docs

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In your Google Doc, create a new row where you want columns
  2. In the first cell of that row, type: Columns
  3. Create a table with 2 columns and 2 rows:
    • Row 1: Header cells (can say "Column" or be descriptive like "Features" / "Benefits")
    • Row 2: Your actual content for left and right columns
  4. Publish your page - the columns will render side-by-side on desktop

Google Doc Markup

When you author columns in Google Docs, the markup looks like this:

Columns

| Column | Column |
|--------|--------|
| Left content | Right content |

AEM converts this to HTML and your block decorator handles the responsive styling.
